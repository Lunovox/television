Decorative LCD TV that displays ad images and server tips. 

Depends:
 * default (mod of standard game)
 * tradelands (optional) → https://github.com/Lunovox/tradelands
 * intllib (optional) → https://github.com/minetest-mods/intllib

Fork of mod:

Developers:

Languages:
 * en ← English (default, no need extra mod).
 * pt ← Potuguese per command [/set language pt]. Need mod 'intllib' to work.
Contact the developer of this mod to add a translate to your language.
